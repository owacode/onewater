Developing Water Professionals

OneWater Academy is proud to be a nonprofit professional learning organization that meets the needs of the water and wastewater industries while working on vital issues of concern to its members and the public.

The digital OneWater Academy platform incorporates advanced technologies like Artificial Intelligence to develop individualized learning plans that are accessible on various devices, on demand.

Content on various topics of relevance to the water sector will be curated through communities of practice, led by subject matter experts from around the globe.
