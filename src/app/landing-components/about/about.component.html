About OneWater

Developing Water Professionals for a One Water Resilient World

The One Water Academy (OWA) will provide a one water knowledge platform to advance resilience. There is a significant need for an educated and experienced workforce for a one water resilient world.

There is a growing need in the industry to develop water professionals at various career levels.

OWA seeks to equip government and utilities, the private sector and academia with the skills and knowledge to prepare, respond and recover from shocks and stresses to the water system. The digital platform incorporates advanced technologies like Artificial Intelligence to develop individualized learning plans which are accessible on various devices on demand as well as enables peer-to-peer learning. Content on various topics of relevance to the water sector will be curated through established Communities of Practices led by subject matter experts around the globe through invitations only.

Vision

The Vision of the One Water Academy (OWA) is to develop a One Water workforce for a One Water resilient world.

Mission

The Mission of OWA is to provide a One Water knowledge platform to advance resilience through a multi-faceted, state-of-the-art digital learning platform to help accelerate and advance knowledge sharing, education, innovation, and capacity building in the water sector.
